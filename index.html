How Projects Are Quoted

Each project is quoted based on length, complexity, and production requirements. Straight text layouts are different from projects with footnotes, charts, tables, layered headings, images, captions, references, or other specialized elements.

Length

Word count, page count, and overall manuscript size affect the scope of the work.

Complexity

Elements like notes, references, tables, charts, callouts, and images increase the level of detail involved.

Structure

Projects with multiple heading levels, special page treatments, or custom layout needs require added care.

Production Needs

Print, digital, or hybrid output can shape file preparation and final deliverables.
